ABOUT US

Profile

Crafty Metaverse is a multi-blockchain based virtual reality metaverse platform to develop the most immersive, imaginative, and addictive gaming experiences. We are providing our gaming community with the near-real-world experience while using their digital avatars. Our ecosystem will be a futuristic, decentralized, and persistent 3D virtual metaverse gaming platform especially for crypto enthusiasts, gamers, artists, and metaverse fans.

OUR MISSION

Vision

Our mission is to create an innovative and engaging metaverse platform that bridges the gap between traditional gaming and blockchain technology. We aim to provide a seamless, immersive experience where players can truly own their digital assets and participate in a thriving virtual economy.

OUR VISION

Future

We envision a future where the boundaries between virtual and physical worlds blur, creating unprecedented opportunities for creativity, commerce, and community. Our platform will be at the forefront of this revolution, offering cutting-edge technology and innovative features that set new standards in the metaverse space.

FAQ

Crafty Metaverse is a blockchain-based virtual reality metaverse platform focused on building a decentralized, persistent online 3D/VR environment that will allow interactions among humans in the form of Avatars.

We use BCMETA tokens as our primary cryptocurrency for transactions within the Crafty Metaverse ecosystem.

BCMETA tokens can be used for purchasing NFTs, participating in games, and accessing various features within our metaverse.

The tokens are built on multiple blockchains to ensure wide accessibility and low transaction fees.

Users can stake their BCMETA tokens to earn rewards and participate in governance decisions.

The total supply of BCMETA tokens is fixed, making it a deflationary asset within our ecosystem.
